How to make the best Hamburger!

Photo by Eaters Collective on StockSnap

This is an example photo of a Hamburger.

Description:

I cook this dish every tuesday, it's my go to meal to make a good impression!

Recipe:

Ingredients

Ingredients are very simple:

  • 250 g of mixed minced beef and pork to make two burgers
  • Half onion
  • A salad leaf
  • A tomato
  • A cheddar slice
  • Ketchup and mayonnaise

Steps:

  1. Put some butter on a pan, make it melt at low heat and add the onion with a ts of sugar, a tbs of balsamic vinegar and a bit of salt.
  2. When the onions are caramelized, take them out and put the two burgers in the same pan where the onions were, cook them both sizes at medium-high heat and then add some wine.
  3. When the alcohol in the wine is evaporated, take out the burgers and cook some buns in the same pan, making it take the sauce that's left from the burgers.
  4. Compose the burger: Lower bread, salad, first burger, slice of cheddar, second burger, ketchup, onions, two slices of tomato and on the upper bread some mayonnaise

The best burger on Earth is ready! There are some variants with bacon and eggs, but the base is this and then you can add your touch!
