How to make the best Lasagna!

This is an example photo of a lasagna.

Description:

This one is a great recipe for the sunday lunches with your family!

Recipe:

Ingredients

The ingredients aren't many, but they all have their great importance:

  • Lasagnas: not the finished meal but just the pasta, possibly made with eggs. You can do this just making pasta dough and spreading it with a rolling pin, and cutting it in a rectangular shape
  • Minced mixed beef and pork, to make the rag첫 with the tomato sauce, a onion, a carrot and a celery.
  • Besciamella: made with butter, flour and milk with some nutmeg and salt
  • Mozzarella and Grana Padano

Steps:

  1. Firstly, we start making the rag첫: cut the celery, onion and carrot in small pieces, put them with some oil in a saucepan and start heating them at medium until they start to golden. When ready, add the minced meat and continue cooking until all the meat becomes brown. Then, add some wine, and make it cook until you can't smell the alchol anymore. Now add sault, black pepper and spices you like (some paprika or hot pepper). Finally, you can add the tomatoe sauce and make it cook at low heat until it dries a bit, to your liking (old grandmas in Italy make it cook up to 3 hours!)
  2. After cooking the Lasagnas in boiling water if needed, you get a baking tray and start composing the lasagna layer by layer: pasta, rag첫, besciamella and cheese; repeat until you use all the lasagnas.
  3. In the top layer, i suggest using only rag첫 and grana, to make it crispy and crunchy
  4. Bake it in the preheated oven for 40 mins at 180 degrees.

And there you have it, a delicious Lasagna for you and your loved ones!
